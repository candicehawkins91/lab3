About

My name is Candice Hawkins, and I am 33 years young, full of energy, and eager to embrace life to the fullest. I was born on January 26, 1991, a date that feels extra special every year when I celebrate with loved ones. I have spent my entire life in the charming city of Alexandria, Louisiana, a place rich with memories, community, and the warm southern atmosphere I’ve come to cherish.

I’ve been fortunate enough to share my life with my incredible husband, who has been my partner for 16 wonderful years. While we’ve shared a journey filled with love and growth for over a decade and a half, we officially tied the knot six years ago, making our bond even stronger. Together, we’ve been blessed with three amazing children who light up our lives and keep us on our toes with their boundless energy and curiosity.

I’ve always had a passion for learning new things, whether it’s discovering a fascinating book, mastering a new skill, or simply exploring the world around me. Some of my favorite pastimes include reading, swimming, and spending quality time with friends and family, which always fills my heart with joy. I also enjoy experimenting with creative and practical ways to save time and money, whether through DIY projects, organization hacks, or finding smarter ways to manage my busy life.
